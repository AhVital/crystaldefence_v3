Our Story

Founded in 2012 in Kiev, Ukraine, Crystal Defence has established itself as a leading manufacturer of UL 752 certified armored glass solutions. Since our inception, we have been dedicated to providing premium protection solutions to businesses and individuals worldwide. Our team of experts combines years of experience in materials science with cutting-edge technology to deliver the highest quality bulletproof glass on the market.

Our Mission

To provide world-class protection solutions that safeguard lives and property while maintaining the integrity and aesthetics of your building. We believe that security should be invisible yet impenetrable, beautiful yet resilient.
