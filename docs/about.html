About Crystal Defence

Since 2012 in Kiev, Ukraine

Our Story

Founded in 2012 in Kiev, Ukraine, Crystal Defence has established itself as a leading manufacturer of UL 752 certified armored glass solutions. Since our inception, we have been dedicated to providing premium protection solutions to businesses and individuals worldwide.

Our mission is to provide superior ballistic protection while maintaining aesthetic appeal and architectural integrity. We combine cutting-edge technology with rigorous quality control to deliver products that stand the test of time.
